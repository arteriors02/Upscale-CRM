How to Use the CRM
Upscale Arteriors — Daily Workflow Guide
Live Connected to Google Sheet
New lead from Meta → Saved in Google Sheet → Appears in CRM automatically
Update Stage in CRM → Google Sheet updates instantly
No manual sync needed — everything is automatic!
Every Morning
Check Today's Follow Ups
The gold banner at the top of Dashboard shows all leads due for follow up today. Start here every morning.
Step 1
📞 Call the Lead
Tap Call → Phone dials → Come back → Select what happened:

No Pickup → Follow up set for tomorrow
Call Back → Pick a time
Connected → Update stage + add note
Not Interested → Moves to Lost
Step 2
💬 Send WhatsApp
Tap WA → Choose a template: Portfolio, Follow Up, No Pickup Fallback, Quotation Follow Up, or Site Visit Confirm.
Step 3
✏️ Update Stage
Tap Edit → Change Stage → Save → Google Sheet updates automatically.
💡 If new leads are not showing — tap Refresh in the sidebar or top bar.
