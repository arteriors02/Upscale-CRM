How to Use

1. Sync / Refresh — Click Refresh to pull latest leads from Meta Instant Forms via Google Sheets.


2. Call Pending — Dashboard shows all New Lead contacts. Call them first and update stage.


3. Update Lead — Click any lead → Edit → update Stage, Call Status, Follow Up Date → Save.


4. WhatsApp — Open any lead → scroll to WhatsApp section → tap template → opens WhatsApp with message pre-filled. Single click.


5. Follow Ups — Today's follow-ups shown at top of Dashboard with full details.


6. Pipeline — Visual pipeline of all stages. Click any card to open lead.
